# Pricing Sources

Use this guide whenever the output mentions price, free tiers, plan limits, or upgrade thresholds.

## Source priority

Prefer sources in this order:
1. official pricing page
2. official billing or plan documentation
3. official help-center article
4. official product announcement if the pricing page is ambiguous

Do not rely on blogs, affiliate sites, or old comparison pages when official pricing exists.

## What to capture

For each service, extract:
- plan name
- billing cadence when visible
- monthly or yearly price
- what is included in the free tier
- the key limit that usually forces an upgrade

## Output rule

Separate:
- confirmed facts from official sources
- your inference about when a team would need to upgrade

Example:
- Fact: "The free plan includes X and Y."
- Inference: "A team usually upgrades once it needs Z."

## Freshness rule

Pricing is unstable information. Recheck it every time.

If the exact price is unclear:
- say that the vendor's pricing was ambiguous
- link the best official page you found
- avoid guessing
